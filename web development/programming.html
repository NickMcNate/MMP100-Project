Description

Nicolas Cage, an abnormality located in [EXPLUNGED], is a Humanoid Entity that possess the ability to imprison any form of object, structure and any organism in a stainless steel cage. This cage is 5 feet bigger in diameter of the target's size. It is best regarded that this entity must not breach containment; as Nicolas Cage is difficult to apprehend after escaping.

Containment

Nicolas Cage's Containment Unit is a 12x12 cell made of galvanized steel; equipped with surveillance cameras to monitor the entity.

There is no access from both outside and inside the containment unit, and there is no need for maintainance either.
